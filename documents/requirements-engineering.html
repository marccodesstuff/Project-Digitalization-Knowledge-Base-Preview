High Level Requirements

Engaging Combat Experience

The game shall deliver a dynamic and fluid action-based combat system alongside a strategic and timed turn-based combat system, allowing players to utilize their skills through strategy and well-timed inputs. AI behavior should adjust based on player actions, and boss battles should provide escalating challenges with distinct phases and mechanics that engage players continuously.

Immersive World Design

The game shall feature an expansive open world that reflects the game’s unique visual style and lore. Players should be able to explore diverse environments that offer a sense of freedom and discovery. Interactive elements such as hidden areas and environmental storytelling, will enhance exploration. The world shall be populated with dynamics NPCs and a day-night cycle that responds to player action, contributing to the immersion and ensuring the world feels alive and reactive.

Impactful Narrative and Meaningful Choices

The game shall offer a branching narrative where player decisions have meaningful consequences on both the main story’s direction and character relationships. The narrative should encourage on both multiple playthroughs by offering divergent paths, multiple endings, and a New Game+ mode that unlocks additional content or altered storylines. Player choices should influence key events, leading to unique character arcs, alternative outcomes, and evolving world states that encourage players to experiment with different decision-making approaches.

Replayability and Player Incentives

The game shall provide incentives for multiple playthroughs by incorporating a range of replayability features such as achievements, hidden contents, and collectibles that encourage exploration and mastery of different aspects of the game. New Game+ should offer added challenges, enhanced difficulty, or new items that carry over from previous playthroughs. Additionally, certain story elements and world interactions should only be available on repeat playthroughs, offering players new perspectives and deeper understanding of the game’s world and characters.

The game shall also incorporate rogue-lite elements to enhance replayability and provide a unique experience in each playthrough. These elements include unlocking permanent character upgrades as well as run-based stat buffs, and some implementation of the nuzlocke system.

Detailed Requirements

Performance

The game shall allow players to transition smoothly between action-based and turn-based combat modes during battles without loading screens or interruptions The system will ensure a fluid gameplay experience where the switch is intuitive and does not disrupt the pacing of the combat. Transitions should be accompanied by visual and audio cues to indicate the change while maintaining performance stability.

It shall also be optimized to run smoothly on all supported platforms without significant frame drops, stuttering, or latency issues that could disrupt the player experience. Optimization efforts will include careful asset management, efficient resource loading, and well-designed animation systems to ensure high performance even in graphically intensive scenes or during complex combat sequences.

Combat System Settings

The game shall provide multiple difficulty settings to accommodate players of varying skill levels, from casual to experienced. Difficulty levels will influence enemy AI behavior, damage scaling, and overall combat challenge. In easier difficulties, combat should focus more on exploration and narrative, with simplified mechanics. While in harder difficulties, players will face more aggressive and intelligent enemies, requiring advanced strategies, positioning, and timing for success.

Accessibility

The game shall include a variety of accessibility options to accommodate players with different needs. These will include customizable controls (e.g., remappable keys, controller support), adjustable difficulty settings for specific aspects of gameplay (such as timing windows for attacks or puzzles), subtitles with scalable text size and color contrast options, and visual/audio aids to support players with visual or auditory impairments. Additionally, features like colorblind modes and input latency adjustments will be considered to enhance inclusivity.
