Project Title

Shattered Eternity

Introduction

Purpose

To provide players with a combat system that seamlessly blends action and turn-based elements, set within a futuristic yet apocalyptic world.

Document Convention

High-Level Requirements

  • Engaging Combat Experience - The game shall deliver a dynamic and fluid action-based combat system alongside a strategic and timed turn-based combat system, allowing players to utilize their skills through strategy and well-timed inputs. AI behavior should adjust based on player actions, and boss battles should provide escalating challenges with distinct phases and mechanics that engage players continuously.
  • Immersive World Design - The game shall feature an expansive open world that reflects the game’s unique visual style and lore. Players should be able to explore diverse environments that offer a sense of freedom and discovery. Interactive elements such as hidden areas and environmental storytelling, will enhance exploration. The world shall be populated with dynamics NPCs and a day-night cycle that responds to player action, contributing to the immersion and ensuring the world feels alive and reactive.
  • Impactful Narrative and Meaningful Choices - The game shall offer a branching narrative where player decisions have meaningful consequences on both the main story’s direction and character relationships. The narrative should encourage multiple playthroughs by offering divergent paths, multiple endings, and a New Game+ mode that unlocks additional content or altered storylines. Player choices should influence key events, leading to unique character arcs, alternative outcomes, and evolving world states that encourage players to experiment with different decision-making approaches.
  • Replayability and Player Incentives - The game shall provide incentives for multiple playthroughs by incorporating a range of replayability features such as achievements, hidden contents, and collectibles that encourage exploration and mastery of different aspects of the game. New Game+ should offer added challenges, enhanced difficulty, or new items that carry over from previous playthroughs. Additionally, certain story elements and world interactions should only be available on repeat playthroughs, offering players new perspectives and deeper understanding of the game’s world and characters. The game shall also incorporate rogue-lite elements to enhance replayability and provide a unique experience in each playthrough. These elements include unlocking permanent character upgrades as well as run-based stat buffs, and some implementation of the nuzlocke system.

Detailed Requirements

  • Performance - The game shall allow players to transition smoothly between action-based and turn-based combat modes during battles without loading screens or interruptions The system will ensure a fluid gameplay experience where the switch is intuitive and does not disrupt the pacing of the combat. Transitions should be accompanied by visual and audio cues to indicate the change while maintaining performance stability. It shall also be optimized to run smoothly on all supported platforms without significant frame drops, stuttering, or latency issues that could disrupt the player experience. Optimization efforts will include careful asset management, efficient resource loading, and well-designed animation systems to ensure high performance even in graphically intensive scenes or during complex combat sequences.
  • Combat System Settings - The game shall provide multiple difficulty settings to accommodate players of varying skill levels, from casual to experienced. Difficulty levels will influence enemy AI behavior, damage scaling, and overall combat challenge. In easier difficulties, combat should focus more on exploration and narrative, with simplified mechanics. While in harder difficulties, players will face more aggressive and intelligent enemies, requiring advanced strategies, positioning, and timing for success.
  • Accessibility - The game shall include a variety of accessibility options to accommodate players with different needs. These will include customizable controls (e.g., remappable keys, controller support), adjustable difficulty settings for specific aspects of gameplay (such as timing windows for attacks or puzzles), subtitles with scalable text size and color contrast options, and visual/audio aids to support players with visual or auditory impairments. Additionally, features like colorblind modes and input latency adjustments will be considered to enhance inclusivity.

  • Game Development Team - This includes developers, designers, artists, and project managers who will rely on the SRS to align their work with the project's objectives. Clear requirements will help them understand their roles and deliverables.

Product Scope

Project Digitalization is an innovative hybrid action and turn-based RPG set in a post-apocalyptic world. The game aims to deliver a unique gaming experience by seamlessly blending fast-paced action combat with strategic turn-based encounters, all wrapped in a compelling narrative that responds to player choices.

Key objectives and benefits include:

  • Engaging players with a dynamic combat system that combines real-time action and turn-based strategy, appealing to a wide range of RPG enthusiasts.
  • Immersing players in a richly detailed world with a complex, branching narrative that encourages multiple playthroughs and fosters community discussions.
  • Providing a fresh take on the RPG genre by incorporating innovative mechanics inspired by successful games and anime series, setting the game apart in a competitive market.
  • Offering high replayability through multiple endings, New Game+ features, and rogue-lite elements, ensuring long-term player engagement and potential for additional content.

From a business perspective, Project Digitalization aligns with our corporate goals by:

  • Establishing our studio as a creator of unique, high-quality gaming experiences that push the boundaries of traditional genre definitions.
  • Targeting a diverse player base, from casual gamers to hardcore RPG fans, potentially expanding our market reach and increasing revenue streams.
  • Creating opportunities for post-launch content and potential franchise development, supporting long-term business growth and sustainability.
  • Showcasing our technical expertise in seamlessly blending different combat systems, positioning our studio as innovative developers in the gaming industry.

Overall Description

Product Perspective

Project Digitalization is a unique action RPG that combines elements from various successful games and anime series to create a fresh and engaging experience. The game draws inspiration from the following key sources:

  1. Combat System: Inspired by The Legend of Heroes: Trails through Daybreak, the game features a dynamic combat system that seamlessly blends action-based and turn-based elements. This hybrid approach allows for both fast-paced, real-time battles and strategic, thoughtful encounters.
  2. World Design: Taking cues from Solar Ash, the game presents a visually striking and surreal world that encourages exploration and discovery. The environment is designed to be both beautiful and integral to the gameplay experience.
  3. Narrative Structure: Drawing from the complex storytelling of Code Geass, Project Digitalization offers a multi-layered plot with political intrigue, personal conflicts, and moral dilemmas. The narrative adapts to player choices, leading to multiple possible outcomes.
  4. World-Building and Lore: Following the example set by The Legend of Heroes series, Project Digitalization features a rich, detailed world with its own history, politics, and cultural nuances that players can gradually uncover throughout their journey.

By combining these elements, Project Digitalization aims to create a unique gaming experience that stands out in the action RPG genre. The game will offer players a deep, immersive world to explore, a compelling narrative that responds to their choices, and a combat system that rewards both quick reflexes and tactical thinking.

Operating Environment

The software will be operational on a Windows-based operating system, with the minimum version being Windows 10 (Steam no longer supports Windows versions below Windows 10). A Linux version of the software is also being considered.

As for portable handheld PCs such as the Steam Deck and the ASUS ROG Ally, the Windows version will do for operating the software.

Design and Implementation Constraints

  • The rendering of game objects of the software should still be visible even though the graphical quality of the software is adjusted by the users.
  • There is no budget for the development of this software as of the writing of this SRS. Because of this, localization of the software is not possible. The only language supported by the software in terms of its UI, dialogue, and audio is English.

User Documentation

The user will be able to learn how to operate the software/game through the tutorials presented in the game. The control scheme will also be visible via the Options/Settings screen (with a visualization of the controller as well).

Assumptions and Dependencies

  • The licensing agreement could change on the game engine used which is Unity. In September 2023, Unity changed their licensing which led to the market share of Unity to drop. The market share of Unity will also affect its licensing in the future.
  • The project will be migrating from Unity 2022 to Unity 6 by the time it will be released. The development team assumes that the project transition will be smooth since the Unity engine has a tool to migrate projects from older versions.
  • The music composer does not have a legal license for FL Studio, and will be using a pirated copy instead. It is assumed that the music composer will be acquiring a legal license once the development of the software reaches its conclusion.

Software Interfaces

Operating System

  • Windows 10/Windows 11 - The game will be optimized for these operating systems, ensuring smooth performance and compatibility with a wide range of hardware configurations. Regular updates and patches will be released to address any OS-specific issues and take advantage of new features or optimizations in Windows.
  • Debian-based Linux - Linux is also being considered as an additional platform for the project. Because of this, testing will also be done on a Debian-based Linux operating system like Ubuntu or Linux Mint.

Tools

  • Unity Engine - The game will be developed with the Unity game engine version 2022, with the intention to migrate to Unity 6 once it releases later in 2024. It will be utilizing the Universal Rendering Pipeline.
  • Visual Studio Code - Visual Studio Code will be used as the primary integrated development environment (IDE) for writing and editing code. Its extensive plugin ecosystem, Git integration, and debugging capabilities make it an ideal choice for efficient development workflow. The team will utilize VS Code's collaboration features to streamline code reviews and pair programming sessions.
  • Blender - Blender will be used for 3D modeling, animation, and rendering of game assets. Its open-source nature and powerful features make it an ideal choice for creating high-quality visual elements that align with the game's unique art style. The 3D models and animations created in Blender will be exported and integrated into the Unity engine for use in the game.
  • FL Studio - FL Studio will be used for music composition, sound design, and audio production. Its versatile tools and intuitive interface make it ideal for creating the game's soundtrack, sound effects, and atmospheric audio. The audio assets produced in FL Studio will be exported and integrated into Unity to create an immersive audio experience that complements the game's visual style and gameplay.
